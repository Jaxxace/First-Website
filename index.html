About $NOT

$NOT is a American rapper who came up in the music industry by using a deep and mysterious voice in mid 2020.

- TRAGEDY +

Following his breakout single "Gosha", $NOT will proceed to drop his first album "- TRAGEDY +." With features from Maggie Lindemann & Wifisfuneral.

Beautiful Havoc

Following his recent album earlier on the year, $NOT would proceed to realease "Beautiful Havoc." His second album, considered his freshman album. This album contains features from, iann dior, Denzel Curry & Flo Milli. This is around when $NOT was really starting to bloom in the music industry. Even capturing Cole Bennett's attention gaining two music videos directed by him.

Tell Em

"Tell Em" was a highly anticipated song during 2021. It shows $NOT teaming up with American rapper Cochise making a song with a very euphoric vibe to it. The song would boost both of the artists popularity due to the song going viral.

Ethereal

At this point in his career, $NOT has a very decent following and patiently waits and promotes his third album, "Ethereal" early 2022. He launches things off with the lead single "Doja" with A$AP Rocky. The song even garnering Doja Cat's attention. $NOT is able to secure features from, Trippie Redd, A$AP Rocky, Joey Bad, Juicy J, Kevin Abstract & Teddi Jones.
